About MIT WPU

With a rich legacy of 40 years in fostering world-class academic excellence and over 100,000 alumni across the globe, MIT-WPU is one of the premier institutions of higher learning in India offering Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Diploma, and Ph.D. programmes.

Uniquely positioned as one of the first universities across the world that offers the highest standard of professional education ingrained with the postulates of peace studies, MIT-WPU enables an inclusive culture of dignity, fairness, and respect amongst its students, grooming them into future-ready, responsible citizens of the world.
